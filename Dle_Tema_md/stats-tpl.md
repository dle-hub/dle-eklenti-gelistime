The output of this section is configured in the stats.tpl file. This section configures the display of overall website statistics.

#### TAG: `{datenbank}`

Total database size

#### TAG: `{news_num}`

Total number of news items

#### TAG: `{news_allow}`

Published news items

#### TAG: `{news_main}`

Published on the main page

#### TAG: `{news_moder}`

Awaiting moderation

#### TAG: `{comm_num}`

Number of comments

#### TAG: `{user_num}`

Registered users

#### TAG: `{user_banned}`

Banned users

#### TAG: `{topusers}`

List of the best authors on the website

#### TAG: `{news_day}`

News items published per day

#### TAG: `{news_week}`

News items published per week

#### TAG: `{news_month}`

News items published per month

#### TAG: `{comm_day}`

Comments published per day

#### TAG: `{comm_week}`

Comments published per week

#### TAG: `{comm_month}`

Comments published per month

#### TAG: `{user_day}`

Users registered per day

#### TAG: `{user_week}`

Users registered per week

#### TAG: `{user_month}`

Users registered per month
