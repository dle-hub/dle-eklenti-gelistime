The output of this section is configured in the searchresult.tpl file. This section configures the templates responsible for displaying news found as a result of a search:
`[searchposts]`
text
[/searchposts]

Outputs found results for news items
`[searchcomments]`
text
[/searchcomments]

Outputs found results for comments
`[fullresult]`
text
[/fullresult]

Outputs results as articles
`[shortresult]`
text
[/shortresult]

Outputs results as titles

Attention: in this template, the same tags are used for the direct output of articles and comments as in the templates for displaying short news and comments.
